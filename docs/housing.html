Housing Data

The dataset includes 311 public housing residents from six public housing projects. Two of the projects were high-rise buildings for seniors and individuals with disabilities, and four projects were for families.

The data were from a study of public housing relocations that occurred in Atlanta (Oakley, Ruel, and Wilson, 2008; Ruel, Oakley, Wilson, and Maddox, 2010).
